#### **Talk**
- **Topic:** Robustness against Release/Acquire Semantics

- **Description:** This paper presents an algorithm for automatically checking the robustness of concurrent programs under C/C++11 release/acquire (RA) semantics. The goal is to verify that all behaviors exhibited under RA are also allowed under sequential consistency (SC). The proposed approach reduces the robustness verification problem to a reachability problem under an instrumented SC model. The authors implement this method in a prototype tool called Rocker and evaluate it on several challenging concurrent algorithms. To the best of their knowledge, this is the first precise method for verifying robustness against a high-level programming language weak memory model.
