2026 Jul 15 :: Making sense of eBPF

Speaker: Pragyansh Chaturvedi

Talk

  • Topic: Making sense of eBPF

  • Description: eBPF is one of the most prominent linux kernel extension techniques in use today. This session aims to familiarize the audience with the eBPF subsystem (of the linux kernel) - particularly its internals. We will breifly touch upon its history and how you can write eBPF programs (available frontend languages). But the real essence would be an overview of the BPF ISA and it’s type system, and the verification step. We will briefly touch upon the JIT compiler backend of the linux kernel. Finally, we will discuss some challenges that the current BPF model poses.
