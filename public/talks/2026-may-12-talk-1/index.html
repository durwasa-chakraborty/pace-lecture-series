2026 May 12 :: GC-Integrated Resource Reclamation for Abandoned Fibers in Multicore OCaml

Speaker: Sagar Biswas

Talk

  • Topic: GC-Integrated Resource Reclamation for Abandoned Fibers in Multicore OCaml

  • Description: How abandoned continuations in Multicore OCaml 5 can leak both fiber stack memory and external resources when they are never resumed. A runtime design that integrates continuation tracking with the garbage collector so unreachable suspended fibers can be detected and safely cleaned up.
