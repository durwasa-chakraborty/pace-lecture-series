PACE Lecture Series: Introduction

Welcome to the PACE Lecture Series page! This platform is dedicated to cataloging monthly talks and lightning talks conducted by the members of the PACE Lab. The primary purpose of this page is to serve as a central repository for all past talks and their details.

Volunteering for a Talk

If you wish to volunteer for a talk, please contact the PACE Lab admin. The format for submitting your talk details and the related guidelines can be found here on the website. As the process becomes more streamlined, the format will evolve to ensure consistency.
